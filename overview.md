# Overview

## What PharmaPath does

PharmaPath helps people build a better first-call pharmacy shortlist and review medication-access context before they make a decision. It is designed to be useful without implying live stock, pickup timing, final price, or transfer success.

## Who it is for

- Patients and caregivers who need a calmer starting point for pharmacy calls.
- Prescribers or care coordinators who want medication context nearby before recommending next steps.
- Builders reviewing a healthcare access product that must stay explicit about its limits.

## Product status

Active private product repository. Public app and methodology pages exist, while source and operational details remain private.

## Public documentation boundary

These docs describe the product experience and high-level technology choices. They intentionally avoid private source structure, deployment internals, provider secrets, database schemas, API implementation, auth/session internals, and client-sensitive records.
