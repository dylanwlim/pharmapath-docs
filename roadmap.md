# Roadmap

## Current direction

Active private product repository. Public app and methodology pages exist, while source and operational details remain private.

## Near-term priorities

- Keep search guidance truthful and avoid unavailable live-inventory claims.

- Expand public education around where direct pharmacy confirmation remains required.

## Public roadmap rule

Roadmap items here are product-facing and may change. Private implementation plans, provider setup, infrastructure work, internal prompts, customer-specific commitments, and unreleased proprietary logic stay in the private repository.
