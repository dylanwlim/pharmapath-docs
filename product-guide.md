# Product Guide

A guide to PharmaPath, which helps users organize medication and pharmacy-pathway context before contacting pharmacies or care teams.

## Main Uses

- Medication context
- Pharmacy-pathway guidance
- Call preparation
- Product updates

## Typical Flow

1. Open the live PharmaPath site.
2. Choose the flow that matches your medication or pharmacy question.
3. Confirm stock, pricing, substitutions, transfers, and care decisions with the appropriate licensed party.

## Best Next Step

Open [https://pharmapath.org](https://pharmapath.org) and use the current on-screen path. These notes are meant to make the live product easier to understand, not replace the guidance shown in the product itself.
